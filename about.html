About the author

First off, my last name is pronounced “mar” [1].

I grew up in Olympia, WA, USA and studied computer science at the University of Washington in Seattle. I live in San Francisco, CA and work in Oakland as a software developer at an energy sensing company called Whisker Labs.

I worked on backend infrastructure at Twitter from 2011 to 2015. I operated a number of the core services that underpin its products and collaborated on open source technologies that help others develop software more flexibly. Many of these projects that I worked on are available on GitHub.

Off the clock, I read, cook, and consume as much music as possible. I contribute to open source projects and have built a few standalone applications as side projects, including a recipe cataloging service and a random invention generator.
