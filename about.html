About the author

First off, my last name is pronounced "mar". It’s the un-anglicized equivalent of Maher and is derived from a Gaelic word meaning “hospitable”.

I hail from Olympia, WA, USA and received a BS in computer science at the University of Washington in Seattle. I currently live and work in San Francisco, CA as a software engineer at Twitter with a focus on the construction and scalability of distributed systems. In my time at Twitter, I've operated a number of the core services that underpin the company's products and collaborated on libraries and tools that help others develop software more flexibly. Many of the projects I work on are open source and available on GitHub.

Over the past decade, I’ve worked as a web developer, as a research assistant on a distributed computing project, and as an intern on the Google Maps team.

Off the clock, I read a lot, cook, and listen to as much music as possible. I've built a few standalone applications as side projects, including a recipe cataloguing service and a random invention generator.
