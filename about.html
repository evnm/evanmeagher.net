About the author

First off, Meagher is pronounced "mar". It’s the un-anglicized equivalent of Maher (as in Bill) and is derived from a Gaelic word meaning “hospitable”.

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, I currently live and work in San Francisco. I work on the Core Systems Libraries team at Twitter, which is fancy org-speak for writing libraries and tools that help Twitter scale and develop products more flexibly. Many of the projects I work on are open source and are available on GitHub.

Over the past decade, I’ve worked as a web developer, as a research assistant on a distributed computing project, and as an intern on the Google Maps team.

Off the clock, I've worked on various open source projects and built a few standalone applications, including a random invention generator and a recipe cataloguing service.
